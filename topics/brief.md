# Olo Company Brain Brief

Build an outward-looking company brain for Olo that can explain the business like a new analyst joining a restaurant-tech investment team.

## Questions this repo should answer

- What does Olo sell?
- How does Olo make money?
- Who buys Olo and who actually uses the software?
- Which external systems and market forces most shape Olo's business?
- Which public claims are strong, which are inferred, and what remains unknown after privatization?

## Current high-confidence picture

Olo is a restaurant technology company oriented around digital ordering, payments, delivery orchestration, and guest engagement for restaurant brands. Public company and acquisition materials repeatedly frame Olo as an open SaaS platform for restaurants rather than a consumer marketplace.
