# Olo risk map

## Main externally visible risks

### Marketplace disintermediation
If restaurant brands decide that marketplace-owned storefronts are good enough, Olo's value proposition weakens.

### Integration fragility
An open, integration-heavy platform gains reach but also inherits complexity and dependency risk.

### Payment fraud and processing economics
Payments can expand monetization while also pulling Olo into fraud, chargeback, and authorization battles.

### Disclosure decline after privatization
The business may remain strategically interesting while becoming harder to observe with confidence.

### Restaurant budget pressure
Because restaurant operators face margin and labor pressure, software spend always competes with other operational priorities.
