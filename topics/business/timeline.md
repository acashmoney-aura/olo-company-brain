# Olo timeline

- **2005:** Olo was founded, according to official acquisition materials.
- **Public company period:** Olo operated as a public company and built investor-relations materials around ordering, payments, and guest engagement.
- **2025 acquisition announcement:** Thoma Bravo announced a deal to acquire Olo for approximately $2.0 billion in equity value.
- **2025 close:** Olo announced the acquisition completed and the stock ceased trading on the NYSE.

## Why the timeline matters

The company brain should treat the public-company era and private-equity era differently because disclosure quality changes materially after the close.
