# Olo Ordering

Olo's ordering product is the foundation of the company brain because it anchors the direct digital-commerce story. Public messaging frames it as a customizable online ordering system for restaurants and virtual concepts, oriented around direct digital sales and operational efficiency.

## Why it matters

This product is likely the wedge that gets Olo embedded into a restaurant brand's stack. Once ordering is core infrastructure, adjacent products like payments, dispatch, and engagement become easier to attach.
