# Olo Engage

Engage is the clearest signal that Olo does not want to remain just an ordering utility. Public messaging frames it as a guest-data and marketing layer that helps brands collect, unify, and act on guest information across the stack.

## Why it matters

If ordering gets Olo into the transaction flow, Engage helps it move up the stack into:
- retention
- segmentation
- lifecycle automation
- cross-channel measurement
- guest-level insight

That makes the company more valuable strategically because it starts owning decision support, not just transaction plumbing.
