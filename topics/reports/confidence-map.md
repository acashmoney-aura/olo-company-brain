# Olo confidence map

## High confidence
- Olo positions itself around ordering, payments, and guest engagement.
- Olo is an open restaurant SaaS platform with a strong integrations story.
- Olo was acquired by Thoma Bravo in an approximately $2.0 billion transaction.
- Official acquisition materials state that Olo serves over 750 restaurant brands, 88,000 locations, and has more than 400 integration partners.

## Medium confidence / inference
- Payments is likely a major strategic expansion vector, not just a side module.
- Dispatch functions partly as a hedge against disintermediation by delivery marketplaces.
- Olo's ideal customer is likely the complex, multi-unit restaurant brand that needs interoperability more than a fully closed stack.

## Unknown
- Exact current revenue mix after privatization.
- Product-level margins and attach rates.
- Detailed roadmap and internal investment priorities under private ownership.
